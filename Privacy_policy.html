1. Introduction

Welcome to Campus Bike Sharing. Your privacy is a priority on our university campus. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website at prajwals.online and the bike-sharing services connected to it.

Quick summary

We use your data to improve campus mobility and keep bikes secure. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Types of data we collect

Personal information

To provide the bike-sharing service, we collect:

  • Identity: full name and student or staff ID.
  • Contact: university email address and phone number.
  • Location: address and city details for emergency contact and incident response.

Technical and usage data

Category Data points
Device data IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
Interaction Pages visited, time on site, click patterns, referrer URLs.
Ride data Pickup and return stations, start and end timestamps, trip duration.

3. How we use your data

We process your data only for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery — verifying your identity, processing bookings, returning bikes, charging fees.
  • Safety and security — detecting fraudulent activity, locating lost or stolen bikes, supporting emergency response.
  • Service improvement — understanding aggregate usage patterns, adding new stations, refining the booking flow.
  • Communication — sending booking confirmations, maintenance notices and important policy updates.
No marketing without consent

We will never use your data for marketing emails or share it with advertisers. The platform is funded by the university, not by ads.

4. How long we keep your data

We apply strict retention limits to minimise the data we hold:

  • Account data: retained for the duration of your membership plus 24 months after closure.
  • Support tickets: up to 24 months for quality assurance and dispute resolution.
  • Server logs: 24 months for security monitoring.
  • Ride history: 36 months for billing reconciliation, then anonymised for analytics.

5. Your privacy rights

You are in control of your data. Under New South Wales privacy law you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to fix anything inaccurate.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Restriction — ask us to pause certain processing.
  • Portability — receive a machine-readable export of your ride history.
Right to erasure

You can delete your account and all associated personal data directly from your profile settings, or by emailing the privacy team below.

6. Contact our privacy team

If you have any questions about this policy or our data practices, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@campusbikeshare.edu
  • Postal address: Student Union Building, Level 2, Room 2.12 — University Main Campus.
  • Jurisdiction: New South Wales, Australia.

For non-privacy questions, the regular Contact page is the fastest route.
