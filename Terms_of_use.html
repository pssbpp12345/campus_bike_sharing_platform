2. User eligibility

Access is restricted to verified university students, staff and faculty. By registering, you confirm that:

  • You are at least 16 years of age.
  • You possess a valid university email address.
  • The account information you provide is accurate and kept up-to-date.
  • You will not share your account credentials with anyone else.

3. Booking & usage rules

15-minute auto-expiry

Pending bookings automatically expire after 15 minutes so other riders aren't blocked from finding a bike.

Time limits

Bikes must be returned within the specified rental window to avoid late-return penalties.

Authorised docks

Bikes must only be returned to official Campus Bike Sharing stations listed on the campus map.

One ride per account

An account may only have one active booking at a time. Sharing accounts is grounds for suspension.

No off-campus rides

Bikes are geofenced to the campus boundary. Crossing the boundary may end the trip and trigger a recovery fee.

4. Charges & payment

Campus Bike Sharing is a pay-as-you-ride service. Sign-up is free; charges only apply when you actively ride a bike.

  • Unlock fee: AUD $1.00 per ride, charged the moment you confirm the booking and unlock the bike.
  • Per-minute rate: AUD $0.20 for every minute of active ride time, billed in whole-minute increments and rounded up.
  • Worked example: a 10-minute ride costs $1.00 unlock + (10 × $0.20) = $3.00.
  • Reservations expire after 15 minutes if you do not pick up the bike — no charges apply to an expired reservation.
  • Recovery fees, damage costs, or fines for breaching these terms are charged separately from the standard ride rate and will be itemised in your account.
  • All prices are in Australian dollars and inclusive of GST. Pricing may be updated from time to time; the current rate is always shown in the booking summary before you confirm.

5. Liability & safety

Users are responsible for inspecting the bike before each ride. If a defect is found, it must be reported immediately using the Flag Maintenance feature in the app before riding.

  • Campus Bike Sharing is not liable for injuries resulting from improper use, ignored maintenance flags, or breaches of road rules.
  • Users are financially responsible for damages caused to the bike during their session, beyond ordinary wear-and-tear.
  • Helmets are recommended at all times; the platform reserves the right to mandate them where required by law.

6. Account termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts for violations of these terms, including but not limited to:

  • Theft, vandalism, or attempted resale of any bike.
  • Sharing account credentials with another person.
  • Repeated late returns or refusal to pay outstanding fees.
  • Harassment or threats made to other riders, staff, or maintenance teams.

Accounts may also be closed at any time at the user's request via the profile settings page.
