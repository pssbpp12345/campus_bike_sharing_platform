Why ride

Built for everyday student life.

Not flashy weekends. Not generic city commuters. Just fast, simple, sustainable transport between your dorm and your next class.

Eco-friendly

Solar-charged fleet, zero emissions, and impact data shared with the sustainability office every term. Every kilometre is a kilometre that wasn't driven.

Safe & reliable

Pre-ride inspections, lights on every frame, and a help button on the map page that pings campus security in one tap. Maintenance staff sweep the network daily.

Reachable everywhere

Thirty-two stations cover every quad, library and lab. Average crossing of campus drops from 18 minutes on foot to under 6 on a bike.
